The Enchanted Question: Solve the Spell!

Answer the magical question correctly to cast the spell and unlock the magic!

One bright afternoon, you and your friends are exploring the enchanted forest near the Wizard’s Tower. The trees here are huge, and the air feels full of magic. As you walk, you spot a glowing path hidden between the trees. Curious, you decide to follow it. The path leads you to an old, secret cave. The entrance is covered in sparkling vines, and strange symbols shine on the walls. You feel a little nervous but also excited. What could be inside? As you step deeper into the cave, you find a giant treasure chest! It’s full of shining jewels and golden coins. But there’s a problem: the chest is guarded by a huge, magical creature with glowing eyes. The creature looks powerful, and you know you can’t open the chest unless you figure out what kind of creature it is. You take a deep breath and get ready to solve the puzzle. Can you figure out what kind of creature is guarding the treasure?
